Preserving Our Heritage

Nkul Ekang Y America is a vibrant community of the Ekang diaspora residing in North America. We share a common language family, a warrior heritage, and a deep-rooted oral tradition centered around the Mvet.

Our organization serves as a bridge between our ancestral traditions and the modern American landscape, providing a support network for newcomers and a platform for professional growth.
