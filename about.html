Our Story & Mission

Dedicated to the unity and progress of the Ekang diaspora in the United States.

Who We Are

Nkul Ekang Y America is more than just an association; it is a community of individuals sharing a common heritage and a common vision for the future. Established to support the Ekang people residing in North America, our organization serves as a bridge between tradition and the modern world.

We provide a support network for newcomers, a platform for professional growth, and a guardian for our cultural identity. Through our various programs, we ensure that the values of our ancestors continue to inspire the next generation of leaders.

Our Core Values

Unity

Fostering a strong sense of belonging and mutual support among all members.

Integrity

Operating with transparency, honesty, and professional ethics in all our endeavors.

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in education, career, and community service.

Heritage

Preserving and celebrating the unique cultural history and traditions of the Ekang people.

Our History

Founded in 2015 by a group of visionary leaders in Washington, D.C., Nkul Ekang Y America began as a small gathering of families. Today, we have grown into a nationwide organization with chapters across major US cities, representing a vibrant and dynamic community that contributes significantly to the American fabric while staying rooted in its origins.
